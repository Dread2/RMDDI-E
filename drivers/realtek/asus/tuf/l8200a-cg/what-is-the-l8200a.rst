Realtek® L8200A Gigabit Ethernet Controller (GbE)
=====================

Overview
--------
The **L8200A** is a ASUS Exclusive Gigabit Ethernet Controller developed by **Realtek Semiconductor Co.**. It is commonly used on ASUS TUF series motherboards.

Package and Physical Characteristics
-----------------------------------
- **Package Type:** QFN-32 (Quad Flat No-Lead, 32 pins)
- **Mounting:** Surface-mount directly on the motherboard
- **Thermal:** Central exposed pad for heat dissipation
- **Size:** Compact, designed for space-constrained motherboard layouts
